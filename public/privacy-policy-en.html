Privacy Policy

The GOAT Meter (“the app” / “we”)

This Privacy Policy describes how The GOAT Meter collects, uses, stores, and protects your data, and what rights you have. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account and identity: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google display name, email address, and a system-generated user ID (UID) from Firebase Authentication, used only for authentication and account management.
  • In-app data: Your chosen team (“warzone”), age range, gender, country/region, and your vote (stance and reasons). This data is used for statistics and your personalized battle report card.
  • Device identifier: To prevent duplicate votes and ensure fairness, we use a device identifier (deviceId) to enforce one vote per device. It is not used for cross-app tracking or ad targeting.
  • Technical and log data: For operation and debugging, we may receive anonymous or aggregated technical data (e.g. crash reports, performance) through Google Firebase, Google AdMob, and similar services.

2. How We Use Data

We use the data above to:

  • Provide sign-in, account, and settings management;
  • Compute global stance statistics and generate your personalized battle report card;
  • Prevent duplicate votes, abuse, and cheating to keep the poll fair;
  • Improve app stability and experience;
  • Show ads provided by Google (e.g. rewarded ads) within the app.

3. Third-Party Services and Storage

The app uses the following third-party services; their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and Firestore database. Your account and vote-related data are stored on Google’s servers with encryption. See Firebase privacy.
  • Google AdMob: In-app ads (including rewarded ads). AdMob may collect advertising IDs and interaction data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google reCAPTCHA / App Check: Used to prevent automated abuse and malicious requests and to protect the service.

4. Data Security and Fairness

We use Firebase and App Check (e.g. reCAPTCHA) to protect data and the service. The device identifier is used only to prevent duplicate votes and ensure fairness; it is not used for cross-app tracking or ad targeting.

5. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete your account and all vote and profile data via “Danger Zone” in Settings inside the app;
  • Use the in-app “Delete Account” option to request deletion of data tied to your account;
  • Contact us (see below) about access, correction, deletion, or any questions about this policy.

6. Data Retention

After you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal and vote data in the app as part of our processes. Some anonymous or aggregated data may be retained where required by law or operations.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice or an updated policy page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the means provided in the app or the developer contact visible on the app’s Google Play listing.
