Privacy Policy

The GOAT Meter (“the app” / “we”)

This Privacy Policy describes how The GOAT Meter collects, uses, stores, and protects your data. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and compliance with app store standards. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Data Collection & Device Permissions

We may collect the following data and request these permissions:

  • Account & Identity: When signing in with Google, we receive your display name, email, and a UID from Firebase Authentication. To optimize sharing, we may mirror your profile picture to our secure cloud storage.
  • Geolocation: To ensure regional poll integrity and assign you to a "Warzone," we request access to your Precise Location (GPS). If denied, we fallback to Approximate Location (via IP address).
  • Media & Storage: We request Photo Gallery/Storage permissions when you choose to save a "Battle Card" to your device or customize your avatar. We only access files directly related to these functions.
  • In-App Data: Includes your chosen warzone, age range, gender, country/region, and your voting stance and reasons.
  • Device Identifiers: We use a unique deviceId to enforce a "one-vote-per-device" rule to prevent poll manipulation. This is not used for cross-app tracking.

2. How We Use Data

We use the data above to:

  • Authenticate users and manage personalized settings;
  • Assign Warzones based on geographic location and generate region-specific Battle Cards;
  • Prevent automated abuse and duplicate voting to maintain poll integrity;
  • Display relevant advertisements provided by Google AdMob within the app.

3. Third-Party Services & Storage

We partner with the following trusted services to process technical data:

  • Google Firebase & AdMob: Handles authentication, database storage, security checks (App Check), and ad delivery. Your account data is stored on Google’s encrypted servers.
  • Geocoding Services: We use OpenStreetMap (Nominatim) and professional IP geolocation services for regional assignment; these services only receive anonymous coordinates or IP addresses.

4. Data Security & Your Rights

We use Firebase App Check and other measures to protect your data. You may at any time:

  • Delete your account and all associated voting records and profile data via the "Danger Zone" in the app's Settings;
  • Request data access or correction by contacting us.

5. Data Retention

Upon account deletion, all personal data and voting history associated with your account will be permanently removed or anonymized on our servers. Some anonymized aggregate data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate operational needs.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, please contact us via the in-app support channels or the developer contact information listed on our Google Play Store page.
